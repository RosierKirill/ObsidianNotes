# **Rogue-like Dark Fantasy – Structure**

## 1. Genre & Setting

- **Genre**: Rogue-like, dungeon crawler.
    
- **Ambience**: Dark Fantasy.
    
- **Procedural generation**: randomly generated dungeons (walls, content, enemies, loot).
    
- **Progression**: each level = increasing difficulty, ending with a boss or mini-boss.
    
- **Dungeon components**:
    
    - **Corridors**
        
    - **Rooms**
        
- **Future environments**: forests, plains, mountains, islands, caves, seas.

## 4. Size Categories & Gameplay

### Humanoids & Monsters

- **Small**: knee height, fast, numerous, low HP.
    
- **Human-size**: like player/NPC, equipped, spawn 1-3 max.
    
- **Large**:
    
    - **Thin**: fast, high damage, low HP, may have projectiles.
        
    - **Muscular**: balanced, may have special attacks.
        
    - **Very muscular**: high HP/damage/armor, slow, can grab or stun player.
        
- **Giant**: bosses/mini-bosses, multi-phase, may summon units.
    

### Other Monsters

- **Small**: dog-sized.
    
- **Medium**: horse-sized.
    
- **Large**: elephant-sized.
    
- Medium/Large can sometimes be used as mounts.
    

---

## 5. NPC

- Species: humans or other humanoids (maveks, molfirs, etc.)
    
- Functions: merchant, doctor, mercenary, etc.
    
- Interaction:
    
    - Trade/give items
        
    - Dialogue
        
    - Can attack player
        
- Size: one head shorter than the player.
    

---

## 6. Dungeon Structure

- **Progression**:
    
    - exploration → combat → loot → boss
        
- **Bosses**:
    
    - giants or special monsters
        
    - multi-phase patterns
        
    - may summon other creatures
